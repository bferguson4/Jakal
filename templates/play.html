How To Play

General Rules

  • You have 5 guesses to guess a random SSBU pro player
  • The available players are the top 50 LumiRankMid-Year2023 players

Hints

  • Green in any column is a match
  • Yellow in the Country means the correct country is in the same continent
  • Yellow in the Rank means the correct rank is within 2 places of the guessed rank
  • Yellow in the Main means the correct main is in the same franchise
